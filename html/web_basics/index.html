Cats

Some of the many cat species are:

  1. Lions
  2. Tigers
  3. Leopard
  4. Domestic cat and etc...

The domesticated cats are wonderful pets. They accompany humans. They do:

  • Play
  • Climb
  • Crawl
  • Meaw
